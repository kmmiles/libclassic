Interface CommonNumberResult

for wow we want at least three types of numbers: base, actual and effective. they can mean slightly different things depending on context.

base - base value e.g. dmg values of in-game spellbook actual - factors in gear, spell coeffients, magic school, etc effective - buffs, debuffs, partial resist penalty, natures grace reduction of castTime, etc
